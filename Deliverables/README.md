# Deliverables

Where the team puts work-in-progress and finished artifacts the principal can review.

When a callsign produces something substantial — a research brief, a draft proposal, a hire workup, a multi-file plan, a domain intent brief — it lands here, **not in PKM** (which is the principal's long-term wiki) and **not in Team Knowledge/** (which is durable SSOT). Deliverables is the team's *working surface*: time-stamped, often dated by folder, sometimes created and discarded across sessions.

## When to use it

- **RECON** delivers a research report → `Deliverables/YYYY-MM-DD-<topic-slug>.md`
- **SCOUT** delivers a hire workup → `Deliverables/YYYY-MM-DD-<role-slug>-hire-research.md`
- **TOWER** drafts a phase/transition proposal → `Deliverables/YYYY-MM-DD-<topic-slug>.md`
- **RELAY** delivers a domain intent brief → `Deliverables/YYYY-MM-DD-<domain>-intent-brief.md`
- A multi-file initiative → one folder per initiative: `Deliverables/YYYY-MM-DD-<initiative-slug>/`

## When NOT to use it

- Principal's life facts (people, projects, goals, habits) → `PKM/`
- Daily journal entries → `PKM/Journal/`
- Reference material the team needs forever (SOPs, Workstreams, Guidelines) → `Team Knowledge/`
- One-time migration phase docs (round-0, Phase-0-audit) → `docs/transformation/` (the archive)
- Operational state (delegations, inventory, credentials) → `state/`

## Lifecycle

Deliverables are working artifacts. They live here while the principal reviews and the team iterates. When a deliverable's content **graduates** — e.g., a proposal becomes ratified policy → a Guideline; a research brief becomes a durable Reference — the durable form is authored under `Team Knowledge/` or `PKM/Reference/`, and the deliverable stays as the historical artifact (or is moved to `docs/transformation/` if it was a one-time migration step).

## Naming

`YYYY-MM-DD-<kebab-case-slug>.md` for single files; `YYYY-MM-DD-<kebab-case-slug>/` for folders. The date is the date of *first delivery*, not the last edit.
