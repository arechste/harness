# Principal — Index

This is the principal's (user's) space. Agents read it to understand *who they serve*, what the principal cares about, and where the principal's own knowledge lives (separate from the agents' shared SOPs).

## Contents

- `.user.yaml` — minimal identity + machine roles
- `autonomy-contract.md` — the living, written agreement of what the team may do on the principal's behalf without per-instance approval; grows via propose→approve
- `About-me.md` — principal background, role, interests, working style
- `Goals.md` — what we're trying to achieve with this whole regime
- `Machines/<host>.md` — per-machine principal-facing notes (NOT the inventory; inventory lives under `state/inventory/`)
- `Reference/<topic>.md` — durable knowledge curated by the principal or filed by RECON; PORTed from `dotclaude/home/memory/source_*` in Phase 2
- `Journal/<YYYY>-<MM>-<slug>.md` — durable insights the principal wants to keep

## Conventions

- Files here are **principal-owned**. Agents may read freely; they should propose edits via a task (not silent edits) unless the SOP they're following explicitly assigns them write authority (e.g., RECON adding a new Reference file).
- Principal/Journal/ is for the principal's lessons. Agents' lessons go in their own callsign folder's journal (Phase 2 extension).
