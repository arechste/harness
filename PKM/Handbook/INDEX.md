## Conventions for this Handbook

- Each page is a one-pager (≲ 80 lines). If it gets longer, split it.
- Audience is *you*, not the team. No callsign-internal jargon without a one-line gloss.
- Whenever a page describes mechanics (a folder, an SOP, a settings file), it links to the source — the Handbook is the friendly index, not the SSOT.
- Out of date? Tell QUILL: "the Handbook section on X is stale" — and they fix it.
