# PKM — Master Wiki Hub

This is the principal's (user's) space — the personal long-term wiki. Agents read it to understand *who they serve*, what the principal cares about, and where the principal's own knowledge lives (separate from the team's shared SOPs in `Team Knowledge/`, which is the BKM).

## Contents

### Identity & agreements
- `.user.yaml` — minimal identity + machine roles
- `About-me.md` — principal background, role, interests, working style
- `Goals.md` — what we're trying to achieve with this whole regime
- `autonomy-contract.md` — the living, written agreement of what the team may do on the principal's behalf without per-instance approval; grows via propose→approve

### Wiki (myPKA-style)
- `[[PKM/My Life/INDEX|My Life]]` — Topics, Habits, Goals, Projects, Key Elements (the five life concepts, flat per file)
- `[[PKM/CRM/INDEX|CRM]]` — People & Organizations, cross-linked from Journal and Projects
- `[[PKM/Documents/INDEX|Documents]]` — markdown stubs pointing at where real documents live (passports, contracts, …); never the documents themselves
- `[[PKM/Images/INDEX|Images]]` — single shared image bucket nested by `YYYY/MM/`; embedded into other entries
- `[[PKM/Journal/INDEX|Journal]]` *(forthcoming)* — daily entries, one file per day, nested `YYYY/MM/`; the inbox of the wiki

### Operational
- `Machines/<host>.md` — per-machine principal-facing notes (NOT the inventory; inventory lives under `state/inventory/`)
- `Reference/<topic>.md` — durable knowledge curated by the principal or filed by RECON; PORTed from `dotclaude/home/memory/source_*` in Phase 2

### Curated documentation
- `[[PKM/Handbook/INDEX|Handbook]]` *(forthcoming)* — instruction for the principal on how harness, the team, the inbox, PKM/BKM, and the autonomy contract work

## Conventions

- **The principal owns final content. The team handles capture and connective tissue** (myPKA's rule). Agents may read freely; they propose edits via a task or per the SOP that assigns write authority (e.g., VAULT/QUILL for Handbook curation; RECON for Reference; Journal capture per the Inbox SOP).
- **SSOT applies inside PKM.** If the same fact appears in two places, the file in the relevant concept folder wins; other entries link to it via `[[wikilinks]]`. No duplication.
- **Concept folders stay flat** (one file per concept). **Date-driven folders nest by `YYYY/MM/`** (Journal, Images).
- `PKM/Journal/` is for the principal's lessons; agents' lessons go in their callsign folder's journal (Phase 2 extension).
- See `Team Knowledge/INDEX.md` for the team's operations side — the BKM.
