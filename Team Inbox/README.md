# Team Inbox

Where you drop raw inputs for the team to process.

Anything you throw in here gets filed. Screenshots, voice memos, business cards, PDFs, half-formed thoughts in a `.md` file, links to articles, a quick end-of-day brain-dump — drop it in, then say "TOWER, process the inbox" (or just "TOWER, here's something new").

## How it works

- You drop a file (or paste content into a `.md` file).
- **TOWER** triages, routes to the right callsign (usually **VAULT** for capture and filing).
- The callsign files it into PKM (life, CRM, journal, documents, images), Team Knowledge (if it's a durable team artifact), Deliverables (if it's something the team will work on into a brief), or state/ (if it's operational).
- Cross-links via `[[wikilinks]]` get added automatically.
- The processed input gets removed from Team Inbox once filed.

The SOP that runs this loop: `[[SOP-process-inbox]]`.

## What goes here

- Screenshots of conversations, business cards, whiteboard photos.
- Voice memos to be transcribed.
- Random ideas you want captured but don't want to file yourself.
- Links to articles for **RECON** to research deeper.
- A quick `.md` brain-dump at end of day for **VAULT** to file across PKM.

## What does NOT go here

- A *task* you want done — file a task under `Team Knowledge/tasks/open/` instead (or just say "TOWER, do X" and the team will file the task).
- A *deliverable* you've drafted for the team to react to — that belongs in `Deliverables/`.
- Anything sensitive (credentials, PII) — see `[[GL-002-credential-custody]]`.

## Convention

If you already know exactly where a note belongs, write it directly in PKM. **Team Inbox is for *"I have something, not sure where, just take it."***
